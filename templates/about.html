    "Spread your knowledge. Spread it using Spreddit®!" --Socrates, probably.


    Spreddit® (in no way a knockoff of Reddit®) is a forum for spreading news.

    You can create an account and make posts, or comment on others' posts.

    You can delete your own posts along with all the comments on them.

    Posts and comments are timestamped; posts appear in order of newest first, while comments are listed in chronological order.

    There is a search box on the homepage that lets you retrieve all posts and comments that contain a certain phrase.

    Go forth, and Spreddit!
